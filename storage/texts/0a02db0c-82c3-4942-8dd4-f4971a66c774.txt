1. HealthTech: Save lives with code.
Description: Build AI for diagnostics, patient care, telemedicine, mental health, or hospital
optimization. From personalized treatment to better care delivery - the future of healthcare is in
your hands.
Specific Challenges:
● AI-Powered Diagnostics: Develop a model to analyze medical images (e.g., X-rays,
MRIs, retinal scans) for early detection of diseases like cancer, diabetic retinopathy, or
pulmonary conditions, aiming for high accuracy in resource-limited settings.
● Personalized Treatment/Drug Repurposing: Create a Gen AI tool that synthesizes
patient-specific health data (genomics, vitals, electronic health records) to suggest the
most effective dosage or identifies existing drugs for new therapeutic uses.
● Mental Health Companion: Build a conversational AI (chatbot/agent) to provide
immediate, non-judgmental support, cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) exercises, or
personalized well-being plans, while escalating critical cases to human professionals.
● Hospital Workflow Optimization: Implement an AI system to predict patient flow,
optimize surgical scheduling, or manage pharmacy inventory to reduce waiting times and
improve resource allocation.

2. FinTech: Money moves fast - your AI should move
faster.
Description: Build solutions for fraud detection, smart investing, financial inclusion, or next-gen
payments. Make finance safer, smarter, and accessible to all.
Specific Challenges:
● Real-time Fraud Detection: Design a deep learning model to instantly analyze
transaction patterns and flag anomalies with extremely low latency, specifically targeting
synthetic identity fraud or money laundering across diverse datasets.
● Generative Investment Strategies: Create a Gen AI model that can synthesize
financial news, market sentiment, and macroeconomic data to generate novel,
risk-adjusted investment portfolios or trading strategies.
● Financial Inclusion for the Underserved: Develop a credit-scoring model that uses
non-traditional data (like mobile usage, utility payments, or community behavior) to
reliably assess creditworthiness for individuals without a formal banking history.
● AI-Powered Compliance (RegTech): Build a tool that uses Natural Language
Processing (NLP) to read and summarize complex financial regulations, automatically
cross-referencing them against a bank's internal policies and highlighting areas of
non-compliance.

3. CyberSecurity: Become the shield of the digital world.
Description: Create AI-powered threat detection, vulnerability scanners, security automation, or
privacy-first tools. Your innovation could stop the next major attack.
Specific Challenges:
● Generative Evasion Countermeasures: Develop an AI that can not only detect
sophisticated polymorphic or adversarial malware but can also generate 'dummy'
network traffic or deceptive responses to confuse and isolate attackers.
● Intelligent Vulnerability Prioritization: Build an AI system that ingests vulnerability
scan reports (CVEs) and asset inventory, using machine learning to predict which
vulnerabilities are most likely to be exploited in the current threat landscape, allowing
security teams to prioritize patching.
● Deepfake and Phishing Campaign Takedown: Create a rapid-response AI that
analyzes incoming communications (email, social media posts) to detect the subtle
linguistic and visual cues of highly convincing deepfake or spear-phishing attempts.
● Security Policy Natural Language Interface: Build a Gen AI tool that allows
non-security experts to query complex security policies or run compliance checks using
simple, conversational language.

4. EduTech: Education is evolving - lead the change.
Description: Create personalized learning tools, skill platforms, accessibility solutions, or AI
tutors. Help bring quality education to every learner, everywhere.
Specific Challenges:
● Adaptive Learning Pathways: Develop an AI that dynamically adjusts curriculum
difficulty, content format (video, text, interactive quiz), and pace based on a student's
real-time performance and cognitive learning style.
● AI Tutor and Conceptual Explainer: Build a Gen AI tool that can take a complex topic
(e.g., quantum mechanics, financial derivatives) and explain it using analogies,
examples, and vocabulary tailored to the user's current knowledge level.
● Automated Content Generation: Create a tool that can instantly generate high-quality,
engaging practice problems, flashcards, or interactive scenarios from existing course
material (e.g., lecture notes or textbook chapters).
● Accessibility for Diverse Learners: Design a system that automatically converts
educational media into accessible formats (e.g., generating accurate captions,
converting text to sign language avatars, or creating screen-reader-friendly descriptions
of diagrams).

5. AgriTech: Grow the future with technology.
Description: Build smart farming systems, crop intelligence tools, supply chain optimizers, or
climate-aware prediction models. Empower farmers and transform agriculture with AI.
Specific Challenges:
● Pest and Disease Identification via Imagery: Build a computer vision model optimized
for mobile use that can instantly and accurately identify crop diseases and pests from a
smartphone photo taken in the field, suggesting immediate, localized treatment
protocols.
● Hyper-Localized Climate & Yield Prediction: Develop a machine learning model that
integrates satellite data, local weather station readings, and historical farm yield data to
provide precise, plot-level forecasts for optimal planting/harvesting windows and yield
estimates.
● Supply Chain Transparency and Waste Reduction: Create a blockchain-integrated AI
system that tracks produce from farm to market, predicting potential spoilage points and
optimizing logistics routes to minimize post-harvest losses.
● AI-Driven Water and Nutrient Management: Build a system that analyzes soil sensor
data, plant health, and weather forecasts to precisely calculate the minimum effective
amount of water and fertilizer required, reducing input costs and environmental impact.
